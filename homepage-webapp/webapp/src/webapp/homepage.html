      Every YouTube video has a unique, 11 character ID. Find your video's ID and enter it here.

      The ID can be found by viewing the video in YouTube, and noticing the URL in the browser window. The section of the URL after the v= is the video's ID:

      You can also see the ID in the URL shown under "share":

      This video's ID is _jZ25X1m458

      Enter the ID in the YouTube Video ID field.

      You may also enter any of the full URLs that you see for the video, and Etudes will extract the ID for you.

      If you don't see your content in the preview:
      • The URL may not be correct.
      • The content's server may be down or slow to respond.
      • The web page URL may have an "http" address. Etudes can only use web pages that support an "https" address. Image URLs may use either.
      • The web page may not support being placed into another site. These cannot be used in Etudes.
      If you don't see your video in the preview:
      • The Video ID may not be correct.
      • The YouTube server may be down or slow to respond.
